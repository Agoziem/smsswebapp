Visions & Missions

Vision

To initiate a paradigm shift in teaching and learning process which engenders relevant intellectual skills for innovation and holistic development; thus providing vitalisaton to communal and national renaissance.

Mission

Our mission is to raise and maintain high standards academically and morally through nurturing of the spiritual, academic, emotional and physical being that will in turn produce creative thinking, applied knowledge, collaborative spirit and leadership skills in a secondary school student.

Values

Teamwork, Integrity, Creativity, Stewardship, Excellence, Passion, Resilience
