Our Tracks

Vision

To initiate a paradigm shift in teaching and learning process which engenders relevant intellectual skills for innovation and holistic development; thus providing vitalisaton to communal and national renaissance.

Mission

Our mission is to raise and maintain high standards academically and morally through nurturing of the spiritual, academic, emotional and physical being that will in turn produce creative thinking, applied knowledge, collaborative spirit and leadership skills in a secondary school student.

Values

Teamwork, Integrity, Creativity, Stewardship, Excellence, Passion, Resilience

About

Welcome to SMSS OMAGBA

St Marks Standard Secondary School is located in a reserved and serene environment fit for learning within Omagba phase 1; is a co-educational, mission secondary school under the episcopal leadership of the diocese on the Niger, Anglican communion; which was founded on 13th September 2010, with F. I Okey as the pioneer principal. We pride ourselves with raising morally sound students who through Godly counsels, technological and innovative schemes such as skill aquisition classes, social activities and other extra-curricular activities that will help the total development of our young ones to take their place in the society

Admissions

Admission Requirements

Things Needed
  1. The approved text-books as contained in the recommended list of books.
  2. Exercise books for junior students includes 5 long notes, 5-80 leaves, 5-60 leaves 15-20 leaves. While for senior include 15 long notes and 15-20 leaves
  3. Revised Standard Version Bible, Ancient& modern and student manual Assembly.
  4. A pair of black container shoe, Navy blue beret for girls, a pair of white socks and white canvas
  5. A wooden locker and wooden back chair (please no iron locker or Seat)
  6. 1 packet of white board maker (black), 2 brooms, 1 ream of A4, 1 Big Tissue
    N/B: Please note that all these are subject to review in subsequent times . Also all the items required must be presented before admission
things not PERMITTED
  1. Students must not bring personal items such as toys, radio, cameras,
  2. phones, games, and any sharp objects etc.
  3. No indiscriminate use of drugs, if a child is on medication the school must be informed.
  4. Improper dressing. dirtiness and lateness
