Welcome to SMSS OMAGBA

St Marks Standard Secondary School is located in a reserved and serene environment fit for learning within Omagba phase 1; is a co-educational, mission secondary school under the episcopal leadership of the diocese on the Niger, Anglican communion; which was founded on 13th September 2010, with F. I Okey as the pioneer principal. We pride ourselves with raising morally sound students who through Godly counsels, technological and innovative schemes such as skill aquisition classes, social activities and other extra-curricular activities that will help the total development of our young ones to take their place in the society
